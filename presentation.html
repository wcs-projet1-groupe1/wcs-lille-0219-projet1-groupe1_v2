Euratechnologie


Description


Born in 2009 in Lille, Euratechnologies is the center of excellence and digital innovation of French Tech. First incubator and accelerator of startups in France and ranked in the top 3 in Europe, Euratechnologies accompanies the development of all its companies. From support to international deployment, it contributes to its success. Let’s talk about numbers, Euratechnologies has :

  • more than 300 companies
  • 200 startups
  • more than 4000 employees
  • 300 supported projects
  • 500 annual events.

History


Stem from the rehabilitation and merging of the two textile companies Le Blan and Lafont, Euratechnologies has large premises with rich history. Following cotton industry’s crisis, the two factories filed for bankruptcy in 1996. In 1998, the Lille Urban Community decided to launch a conversion project called Euratechnologie. It will be inaugurated as we know it on March 26, 2009. The adventure begins in 2006 with the Microsoft and Cap Gemini's establishment, at the time Euratechnology has 25 companies and 300 employees.

Start by Euratech

With incubation program to pass the idea to the prototype in 80 days, Start by Euratech is an ideal accompaniment for young entrepreneurs. It includes about fifteen thematic workshops, entrepreneurs meetings, individualized coaching, entrepreneurs sponsorship and access to technology partner offers.

More information :


Scale by Euratech

In business support, Euratechnologies has created the Scale by EuraTech acceleration program. It consists of a 9-month program divided into two parts :

  • 3 months on the strategy and structure of the company
  • 6 months on the "go-to-market" to work sales, negotiation, marketing, …
It also provides young entrepreneurs with a vast network within the digital ecosystem.

More information :


Grow by Euratech

Once the company are launched, it is possible to settle within the premises of Euratechnologies through the program Grow by Euratech. Available spaces range from 24 m2 up to 5 ha. Companies benefit from several shared services such as meeting rooms, data centers, auditorium, conference rooms, etc.

Opportunities


Dedicated to children aged 6 to 15, EuraTech'Kids is a set of introductory workshops on coding and robotics. It represents an opportunity to introduce children to the field of digital possibilities. it contains a robotic cycle and a Scratch coding cycle. The sessions take place during the year and the school holidays.
